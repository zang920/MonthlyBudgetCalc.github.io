Monthly Salary Budget Calculator

This program splits your montly earnings into 3 categories for best budgeting practices.

50% should be allocated to Needs such as:

  • Housing
  • Food
  • Utilities
  • Healthcare
  • Insurance

30% should be allocated to Wants such as:

  • Casino/Lottery
  • Personal care
  • Entertainment
  • Hobbies
  • Subscriptions

20% should be allocated to Savings such as:

  • Emergencies
  • Savings
  • 401k
  • Investments
